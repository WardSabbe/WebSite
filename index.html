Who is Ward?

Meet Ward, a young designer whose passion for design extends beyond traditional boundaries. With a specialization in lighting design, Ward has always been intrigued by the transformative power of light and its ability to shape environments and evoke emotions.

In addition to his expertise in lighting design, Ward is deeply fascinated by stem-based applications, particularly in the area of Arduino and similar technologies. He sees the potential of these technologies to seamlessly integrate with design, enriching user experiences in innovative ways.

Ward's innovative approach extends beyond conventional practices. Drawing from his hands-on experience with Arduino, he not only crafts captivating lighting installations but also leads stem-focused workshops and develops kits tailored for diverse spaces. His collaborative mindset and unwavering drive to innovate make him an invaluable asset in projects that demand a harmonious blend of design and technology.

In summary, Ward is a multifaceted designer with a relentless passion for both lighting design and stem-based applications. His ability to seamlessly integrate technology with design, coupled with his dedication to pushing creative boundaries, positions him as a dynamic force in the design industry.
